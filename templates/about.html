Our Story

In 2025, I saw how unpredictable monsoons badly affected my dadâ€™s farm in rural India. Even though he has many years of farming experience, he could not decide when to plant crops or which crops would survive because the weather had become very unpredictable due to climate change.

That year, our rice crop failed because of an unexpected drought. After that, sudden heavy rains and floods destroyed the little crop that was left. Watching my dad struggle with losses and financial pressure was very painful. Traditional farming knowledge was no longer enough to fight modern climate problems.

In 2026, I decided to build AgriPredictor. By combining real-time weather data, soil information, and machine learning trained on agricultural data, I created a tool to help farmers make smarter decisions.
